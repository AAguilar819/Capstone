Here you can buy our packages for your own needs if necessary. Every month, 200 more packages are made availible to sell, up to 400 available at once. The prices will vary depending on the products inside the packages. The 200 packages will start with 50 of each type of package and will vary on how much of a type of package is purchased. If the price is higher than expected, please remember the costs to get these items and the increase that we need to charge to stay running. We thank you for your time and your purchase.
